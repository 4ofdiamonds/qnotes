- Solve for n (n is a natural number)
- $nCr=\frac{n!}{(n-r)!r!}$
	- a) $nCn-2=10$
		- $\frac{n!}{(n-(n-2))!n-2!}=10$
		- $n! =10(2)!n-2!$
		- $n! =20(n-2)!$
		- $n(n-1)(n-2)! =20(n-2)!$
		- $n(n-1)=20$
		- $n^2-n-20=0$
		- $n=5$
	- b) $4(nC_{2})=n+2C_{3}$
		- $4(\frac{n!}{(n-2)!2!})=\frac{n+2!}{(n+2-3)!3!}$
		- $n(n-1)=\frac{(n+2)(n+1)(n)}{6}$
		- $6n^2-6n=(n+2)(n+1)(n)$
		- $6n^2-6n=n^3+3n^2+2n$
		- $n^3-3n^2+8n=0$
		- $n=0$
	- c) $(n+1)C_{3}=nC_{2}$
		- $\frac{(n+1)!}{(n+1-3)!3!}=\frac{n!}{(n-2)!2!}$
	- d) $nP_{4}=60(n-1C_{3})$
