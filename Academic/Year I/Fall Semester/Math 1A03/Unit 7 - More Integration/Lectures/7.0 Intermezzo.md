# 5.5 The substitution rule
$$\begin{align}
\text{If} u=g(x) \\
\int f(g(x))g'(x)\,dx & =\int f(u)\,du
\end{align}$$
$$\begin{align}
\int_{a}^b f(g(x))g'(x)\,dx=\int _{g(a)}^{g(b)}f(u)\,du
\end{align}$$
