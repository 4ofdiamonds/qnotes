## 2D Motion and Calculus
- An object moves in a plane such that its velocity as a function of time is
$$\begin{align}
\vec{v}=4t \hat{x}+ 2 \hat{y}
\end{align}$$
- If the object is at the position (1, 0) m at t=0, where is it at t=3s?
$$\begin{align}
v_{x} & =4t &  v_{y} & =2 \\
x & = \int 4t \,dt  & y & =\int 2 \,dt\\
x & =2t^2+x_{0}  & y & =2t+y_{0}\\
x(t) & =2t^2+1  & y(t) & =2t\\
x(3) & =2(3^2)+1  & y(3) & =2(3)\\
x(3) & =19m & y(3) & =6m
\end{align}$$
